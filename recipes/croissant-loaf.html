Croissant Loaf

Description

An easy way to use old pastries to make a delicious new dessert

A very versatile recipe and you can substitute jam with lemon curd or chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Croissants
  • 78g of fruit jam
  • 78g of ricotta
  • 118g of blueberries
  • 3 eggs
  • 177g of double cream
  • 59g of milk
  • 2 Tbsp of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Align a loaf tin with parchment paper.
  2. Slice Croissant into quarters and layer the bottom half onto the loaf tin.
  3. Spread over ricotta and jam, then half of the blueberries.
  4. Layer rest of croissant.
  5. Mix eggs, cream, milk, and, vanilla extract into a separate jug.
  6. Pour over the croissants and let sit for 25 minutes. (Add extra blueberries on top)
  7. Bake at 180 degrees for 35 - 45 minutes.
