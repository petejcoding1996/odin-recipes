Banana Bread

Description

A banana bread and drizzle cake hybrid, this is a quick, easy and tasty loaf, which will leave family wanting more.

This quick recipe can be frozen. It's great if you need to use your overripe bananas, too.

Ingredients

  • 140g soft butter
  • 140g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 140g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 very ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 50g icing sugar
  • handful dried banana chips
